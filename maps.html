The Power of Custom Maps

This map is more than a technical showcase; it represents a fusion of Digital Marketing strategy and Geospatial Data Analysis. By utilising tools like Leaflet.js and custom-styled KML data, I demonstrate how businesses can transform raw location data into an immersive, branded experience.


Why Custom Maps Matter?

In a digital-first economy, the "Find Us" page is often the first point of physical contact. A generic map is a missed opportunity. Custom mapping enhances customer experience by:

  • Contextual Storytelling: Guiding customers through specific routes or points of interest that align with your brand's narrative.
  • Reduced Cognitive Load: Filtering out the noise of standard maps to focus only on what the customer needs to see.
  • Brand Consistency: Maintaining a unified aesthetic, such as this RDR2-inspired interface, the old world sepia theme, which builds stronger brand recall.

My Passion for the Intersection

As a Digital Marketing Specialist with a background in Applied Business Analytics, I am passionate about making data "human."
